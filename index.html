Who We Are

Rooted in Scripture, led by the Spirit

Truth Unveiled Ministries was founded on a simple conviction: when God's Word is faithfully taught and His presence is genuinely sought, lives are changed.

Vision Statement

To see lives awakened to God's truth and transformed by His Word and Spirit.

Mission Statement

Truth Unveiled Ministries exists to unveil the truth of God's Word through sound biblical teaching, fervent prayer, and the ministry of the Holy Spirit; guiding all who seek Him into deeper intimacy with Christ.

What We Believe

Statement of Faith

These convictions form the doctrinal foundation of everything we teach, practice, and pursue together as a ministry.

“if I delay, you may know how one ought to behave in the household of God, which is the church of the living God, a pillar and buttress of the truth.” 1 Timothy 3:15 (ESV)

  1. 01

    We believe there is one true God, eternally existing in three Persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

  2. 02

    We believe in the absolute sovereignty of God over creation, revelation, redemption, and final judgment.

  3. 03

    We believe in the divine inspiration and full authority of the Holy Scriptures as the Word of God. The Bible stands supreme in all matters of faith and conduct.

  4. 04

    We believe that the gospel is, first and foremost, God's gospel. Its authority and truth rest solely on Him. It is the power of God for the salvation of everyone who believes, with no distinction. (Mark 1:14; Romans 1:1, 16)

  5. 05

    We believe in the full deity of the Lord Jesus Christ, the incarnate Son of God — His perfect and sinless humanity, His earthly ministry, His atoning death on the cross, His bodily resurrection, His ascension into heaven, and His present reign as Lord over all.

  6. 06

    We believe that Jesus Christ is the only way of salvation and the sole mediator between God and humanity. (John 14:6; Acts 4:10-12; 1 Timothy 2:5)

  7. 07

    We believe in the one holy, universal Church, which is the Body of Christ, composed of all true believers.

  8. 08

    We believe that the Church is under divine command and obligation to preach the gospel to every person. (Matthew 28:18-19; Luke 24:47)

  9. 09

    We believe that all human beings have sinned and fall short of the glory of God.

  10. 10

    We believe that personal faith in the Lord Jesus Christ — the divine Word, the eternal second Person of the Trinity, who is co-equal and co-eternal with the Father and the Holy Spirit — is essential to salvation. This faith includes a humble acknowledgment of our sin and utter need, together with wholehearted trust in Christ and His finished work.

  11. 11

    We believe that justification is God's gracious act of undeserved mercy, by which He pardons the sinner of all guilt and declares him righteous in His sight. This justification is received by faith alone.

  12. 12

    We believe that the Holy Spirit applies the redeeming work of Christ to the believer. He convicts of sin, produces repentance toward God and faith in Jesus Christ, indwells the believer, progressively transforms the believer into the likeness of Christ, and empowers him or her for witness and service in the world.

  13. 13

    We believe that the Lord Jesus Christ will return personally and visibly to judge all mankind.

  14. 14

    We believe the true role of prophecy within the Church is to point the Body of Christ toward its ultimate goal: exalting Jesus Christ, edifying the community of believers, and actively preparing His followers for His literal return.

What We Stand On

Seven Core Pillars

These convictions shape everything we teach, practice, and pursue together as a ministry.

1

Biblical Teaching & Doctrine

Focus on accurate teaching of Scripture and sound doctrine to ground believers in truth.

  • 2 Timothy 3:16–17
  • Titus 2:1

Prayer & Spiritual Formation

A strong emphasis on prayer, devotion, and cultivating a life shaped by God's presence.

  • 1 Thessalonians 5:17
  • Luke 18:1
3

Worship & Presence of God

Creating environments where God is honored and His presence is central.

  • John 4:23–24
  • Psalm 22:3
4

Fellowship & Community

Building relational unity, accountability, and shared life among believers.

  • Acts 2:42
  • Hebrews 10:24–25
5

Spiritual Growth & Transformation

Helping believers grow in Christlikeness and live transformed lives.

  • Romans 12:2
  • 2 Corinthians 3:18
6

Evangelism & Outreach

Reaching unbelievers with the Gospel and expanding the Kingdom of God.

  • Matthew 28:19–20
  • Romans 10:14
7

Leadership Development & Empowerment

Raising and releasing leaders for ministry, service, and influence.

  • 2 Timothy 2:2
